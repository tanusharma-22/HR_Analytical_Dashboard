Dataset: 1,470 employee records
Tools: Power BI, Power Query, Basic DAX
Built an interactive HR analytics dashboard to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary trends, and tenure patterns.
Calculated key KPIs including attrition rate (16.1%), average age (37 years), average salary (6.5K), and average tenure (7 years).
Identified highest attrition among employees aged 26–35, earning ≤5K salary, and within 1–2 years of tenure, indicating early-stage retention issues.
Analyzed attrition by education, gender, salary, years at company, and job role, highlighting higher turnover in Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives.
Provided actionable insights suggesting improvements in early engagement, compensation, and career growth strategies to reduce employee turnover.
